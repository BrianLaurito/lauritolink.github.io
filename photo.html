This is my photo project. For this project, we were assigned to take 4 different photos, with one being outdoors and one being indoors. Then, we needed to make edits to the photos in Photoshop, and keep track of all the edits we made and write them down for the final product.

Overall, I enjoyed this project because I had creative freedom to take the photos myself, as well as edit them as I saw fit. I learned some new techniques in Photoshop during this project as well.
