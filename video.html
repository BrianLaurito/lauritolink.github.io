This is the finished video created by my group

This is my groups video project. For this project, we were assigned with making a 60-75 second video about somehting on campus. My group decided to talk about Osburn Hall. We started by making a script and storyboard, and writing down our ideas for what we wanted the video to look like. Once we started filming, we realized changes to the script were necessary in order to hit the required time limit.

Overall, this was a fun project that allowed me to meet and talk to new people. I learned a lot about video making and editing.
